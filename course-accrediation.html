Accreditation Process

It is our goal to make this procedure as simple as possible for you to follow and finish. Make sure your legal status is up to date as per the requirement of your country. The process of accreditation is as below:

Email us [info@hrboard.uk to get the application form

Fill up the form and submit to us

BHRC accreditation board will review and if required they will communicate

Once the BHRC accreditation board is satisfied with the documents and process, they may conduct an on-site or online visit to your site

Once the visit is satisfactory BHRC will issue the invoice

Once the payment is done, BHRC accreditation Board will process your application based on some formal procedure

Once all are ok, BHRC will issue the accreditation certificate in favor of the institute

BHRC will guide the institute to start with the accreditation services.
