BHRC Standard: Institution

Board of Human Resource Certification plays a role to ensure standard HR practices globally. Institutes, colleges, and universities offering Human Resource degrees and qualifications should have a standard. BHRC is partnering with these institutes to establish and maintain this global standard for HR institutions. Institutes can use the BHRC stamp while maintaining the standard established by BHRC for any institute.

BHRC Standard: Course

Any courses or qualifications or degrees relating to Human resources should maintain a standard.

BHRC established a standard to maintain by the institutes globally. BHRC stamp can be used if any institutes offer HR qualifications or courses based on the standard of BHRC.
