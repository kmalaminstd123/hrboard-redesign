Our Introduction

BHRC Standard

BHRC standard is a UK standard for HR professionals at different levels of the profession. Based on thorough research and round table discussion among a number of expert HR professionals and educators across the globe, BHRC has established some standards [code and competencies] for HR institutes, their courses and the individual HR professionals for different level.

What We Offer You

Operational Standard

All institutes must show the creditability and capacity to operate programs accredited by BHRC. They must comply with a minimum standard of operation regarding Faculties, other facilities, etc.

Compliance Standard

All Institutes must follow the Business Ethics

Customer Compliance

Institute must have all documentation regarding customer compliance. for example, Customer complaint management documents

Data Compliance

All institutes must follow Data compliance in terms of property right, information sharing compliance etc.

BHRC Competency Framework for HR Professional

Personal Competency indicates the demographic and characteristics of HR professionals. Particularly focusing on the Humanitarian, ethical, integrity, values, etc of a person

Professional Competency indicates the functional competency of HR professionals like the competency required to perform the function of Talent Management, Recruitment, Performance Management, etc. Also, it focuses on the Legal aspect of a particular country/ international law which is connected with employment law.

Leadership Competency indicates the leading capability of a particular person. It also focuses on different leadership styles, Innovation, Change Management, etc.

Entrepreneurial competency indicates the competency that is required to understand and contribute lead any business or its part successfully. For example Financial Expertise, Financial ratio, Business acumen, Business process, Business models, etc

Why BHRC?

It's good that you have a question in mind as to why you should go with BHRC. Well, it's pretty simple, we, BHRC, is for Quality certification, Standard HR practices, and Availability of HR Experts. As an Institute, BHRC helps you to prove your credibility towards learners and corporates. As a professional BHRC helps you to prove your professional credentials and expertise in a specific domain of Human resources.

BHRC global self-regulated body for HR. Establish the standard for HR professionals and accredit institutes worldwide, ensuring standardized expertise and practices across the globe.
