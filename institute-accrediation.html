Process

Process of Accreditation

It is our goal to make this procedure as simple as possible for you to follow and finish. Make sure your legal status is up to date as per the requirement of your country. The process of accreditation is as below:

Apply for Accreditation through online or contact us via email [info@hrboard.uk] for accreditation form

The Accreditation Council will primarily assess the application form. If they find the institute eligible, they will contact you for further details for assessment purposes. They will also issue invoice. Once the invoice is paid, the council will start the formal assessment

If the inspection is conducted successfully, you will be contacted with a congratulatory message. Otherwise, if it doesn't, we'll let the institution know why accreditation is doubtful at this time and offer advice and information on how to address the issues we uncover.
