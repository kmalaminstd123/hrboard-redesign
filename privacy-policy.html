Privacy Policy

Introduction

The Board of HR Certification Institute (BHRC) is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients, members, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we share it, and your rights in relation to your personal data. By using our services or visiting our website you agree to the terms below.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information in several ways, including when you visit our website, register for a course, apply for accreditation, or contact us. Types of information we collect include:

  • Personal identification information: name, email address, mailing address, phone number and contact details.
  • Professional information: job title, employer, qualifications and other employment-related details.
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers and other technical data collected automatically when you access our site.
  • Usage data: pages visited, time on site, actions performed and other analytics describing how you interact with our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing and managing services: delivering certifications, course enrolment and accreditation services.
  • Communication: sending administrative messages, service updates and, where permitted, promotional materials.
  • Service improvement: analysing usage to improve and develop our offerings and website.
  • Compliance: responding to legal obligations and regulatory requests.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: trusted third parties who perform services on our behalf (e.g., certificate fulfillment, hosting, marketing). They are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.
  • Legal requirements: when required by law or to respond to lawful requests by public authorities.

4. Security of Your Information

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures (such as encryption, access controls and secure servers) to protect personal data. However, no online transmission or storage can be guaranteed 100% secure. If a breach affecting your data is detected, we will act in accordance with applicable law.

5. Your Rights

  • Access & update: request access to, or correction of, your personal information.
  • Opt-out: unsubscribe from promotional communications.
  • Request deletion: request erasure of your personal data subject to legal or contractual obligations.
  • Restrict processing: ask us to limit how we process your data in certain situations.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@hrboard.uk. We will respond as required by applicable law.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality and analytics. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings; however disabling cookies may affect site features.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post the updated policy on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact Us

  • Email: info@hrboard.uk
  • Address: Board of HR Certification (BHRC)

This policy is provided as general information and does not create contractual rights. For jurisdiction-specific privacy rights or to make a formal request, please contact us.
